This is a sandbox application where you can play with a basic, manually-tuned Rotational PID Controller.

The goal of this controller is to match the angle of the ball to the target angle of the system

The target angle is defined by the mouses angle relative to the center of the larger circle. It uses a nested pid to control the angular acceleration and angular velocity of the red circle.
